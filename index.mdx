## What is Nana?

Nana is a feature-rich Discord bot built with a soft aesthetic and powerful capabilities. From moderation and anti-nuke protection to fully customizable autoresponders and embed builders — Nana brings everything your server needs in one place.

> *powerful features. soft aesthetic. made with love for your server ♡*
