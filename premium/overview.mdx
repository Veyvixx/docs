Nana Premium unlocks higher limits, exclusive features, and a more personalized experience for both you and your server. Support development and get more out of Nana.

## Tiers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Tier 1" icon="seedling">
    **Entry-level premium.** More embeds, autoresponders, buttons, and access to the embed and AR inventory. Plus `/customize` for custom palettes and emojis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tier 2" icon="leaf">
    **Everything in Tier 1**, plus a higher limit on all features and direct developer support.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tier 3" icon="tree">
    **Everything in Tier 2**, plus a private custom Nana bot configured just for your server.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Checking Your Status

Use `/premium` to see your current tier, expiry date, and a full list of your perks.

If you don't have premium yet, `/premium` will show you all available tiers and how to get started.

## Getting Premium

Join our [Patreon](https://patreon.com/NanaBotDis) to subscribe to a premium tier. After subscribing, use `/activate premium` to apply your benefits to a server.
