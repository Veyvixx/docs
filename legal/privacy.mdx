<Info>
  This Policy describes how the Nana Team collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Bot. It is drafted in compliance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.
</Info>

## § 1 — Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all users who interact with Nana ("the Bot") in any Discord server or context. By using the Bot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

## § 2 — Information We Collect

Nana collects and processes only the **minimum amount of data necessary** for the Bot to function. This is limited to:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Discord User IDs | Moderation case tracking, autoresponder logic, permission management |
| Discord Server (Guild) IDs | Linking configuration settings, premium status, mod logs, and autoresponders to the correct server |

We do **not** collect, read, store, or process message content beyond what Discord's API provides in the context of a direct bot interaction or command invocation. We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personal identifiers beyond those listed above.

## § 3 — How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

- Delivering the Bot's features and commands accurately and reliably
- Maintaining moderation case records and server configuration tied to Guild IDs
- Verifying Premium subscription eligibility linked to server or user identifiers
- Enforcing bans, restrictions, and other moderation decisions
- Diagnosing technical issues and maintaining service stability

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond delivering the Bot's core functionality.

## § 4 — Data Sharing & Third Parties

<Warning>
  The Nana Team does not sell, rent, trade, or share user or server data with any third party under any circumstances. This commitment is absolute and unconditional.
</Warning>

The sole exception is where we are compelled by a valid, legally binding order from a competent authority under applicable EU law. In such cases, we will disclose only the minimum data required to comply with that obligation.

## § 5 — Data Storage & Security

Nana's data is hosted on third-party cloud infrastructure to ensure reliability and availability. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data is retained only for as long as it is necessary to provide the Bot's services or to comply with applicable legal obligations.

## § 6 — Data Deletion Requests

At this time, we do not offer a formal user-initiated data deletion process. The data we store is minimal — User IDs and Server IDs — and is used solely to operate the Bot's core features.

**Removing the Bot from your server** will cease any further data processing associated with that server's Guild ID. For specific concerns regarding stored data, please contact us using the details in § 11.

## § 7 — Children's Privacy

Nana is not directed at children under the age of **thirteen (13)**. We do not knowingly collect data from users under this age. If we become aware of any such data, we will take prompt steps to remove it in accordance with applicable law.

## § 8 — Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or amend this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be announced via the official Nana Support Server and reflected in the updated documentation. The effective date at the top of this Policy will be revised accordingly. Your continued use of the Bot following any amendment constitutes your acceptance.

## § 9 — Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user within the European Union, you may hold certain rights under the GDPR with respect to personal data processed about you. Given the limited nature of data we collect, these may include:

| Right | Description |
|---|---|
| Right to Access | Request information about what data we hold |
| Right to Rectification | Request correction of inaccurate data |
| Right to Object | Object to certain processing activities |

To exercise any applicable rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond in good faith and in accordance with our legal obligations.

## § 10 — Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **European Union**, including the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679. Disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the competent courts within the European Union.
